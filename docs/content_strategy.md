# Content Strategy & UI Design - Chestia-web

## 1. Landing Page Structure

### A. Hero Section
**Visual Goal**: Impactful, clean, dark-themed.
-   **Headline**: "Unlock Your Kitchen's Potential"
-   **Sub-headline/Slogan**: "Chestia turns your leftover ingredients into Michelin-star ideas. Stop wasting, start creating."
-   **CTA Button**: `[ Generate Recipe ]`
-   **Visual**: A high-contrast, cinematic plate or abstract ingredient composition in the background (dimmed).

### B. How It Works (3 Steps)
**Visual Goal**: Simple, clear iconography (glassmorphism style).

1.  **Step 1: Tell Us What You Have**
    -   *Text*: "Input the ingredients currently in your fridge or pantry. No detail is too small."
    -   *Icon Concept*: A sleek search bar or basket icon.
2.  **Step 2: The Magic Happens**
    -   *Text*: "Our AI analyzes flavor profiles to construct the perfect meal from your available stocks."
    -   *Icon Concept*: A glowing brain or sparkling whisk.
3.  **Step 3: Cook & Impress**
    -   *Text*: "Follow limits step-by-step instructions to create a dish that looks and tastes professional."
    -   *Icon Concept*: A chef's hat or a steaming dish.

### C. Footer / Navigation
-   **Links**: "Who we are" | "Contact us" | "FAQ"
